## AI Capex Macro

先判断总水位，再看细分环节。若美国 AI capex 从继续上修变成审慎，A 股硬件链的估值会先受压；若总水位仍上修，重点就是找哪一环节继续吃到订单和盈利预测上修。

| 维度 | 当前判断 | 对 A 股硬件链的含义 |
|---|---|---|
| 总水位 | 总水位仍上修，但市场开始同时交易 ROI、融资成本和资源约束。 | 支撑光模块、AI PCB、存储接口、电力/散热等核心链条，但不能自动推出所有二阶映射都有机会。 |
| 风险温度 | 中高 | 风险不是订单立刻消失，而是高 PE/远期故事先被压估值；进一步加息或长端利率上行会放大这个压力。 |
| 传导顺序 | Hyperscaler capex -> GPU/ASIC 集群 -> 网络/PCB/内存带宽 -> 电力/散热/资源约束 | 中际最直接吃网络瓶颈，沪电吃高速 PCB 复杂度，澜起吃 MRDIMM/Retimer/CXL 等架构升级。 |
| 核心证伪 | Capex 指引停止上修、AI ROI 被质疑、融资型数据中心项目放缓、长端利率继续上行 | 出现组合坏信号时，高拥挤主线先做风险复盘，不再只看静态 PE。 |

## Theme Temperature

Hot 不代表不能涨，但不能叫 emergent；Warming 才适合继续找标的；Early 只是线索，需要人工验证。

**Hot Consensus：只做趋势/超预期复核，不当 early theme**
- 1. AI Server / GPU Infrastructure：高拥挤，Direct AI capex layer. Demand is clear, but market awareness is very high.
- 2. HBM：高拥挤，HBM is a known core bottleneck for AI training and inference memory bandwidth.
- 3. DRAM / NAND / Enterprise SSD：高拥挤，HBM capacity pressure spills over to DRAM, NAND and enterprise SSD pricing and supply.
- 4. Memory Interface / Retimer / CXL：高拥挤，AI server memory bandwidth and interconnect complexity drive DDR5 RCD/MRCD/MDB, PCIe Retimer and CXL demand.
- 5. Optical Modules 800G/1.6T：高拥挤，GPU cluster scaling drives 800G/1.6T optical module demand. This is a known bottleneck.
- 6. CPO / Silicon Photonics / OCS：高拥挤，Networking bottlenecks migrate from pluggable modules toward CPO, silicon photonics and OCS.
- 7. High-speed PCB：高拥挤，AI servers and high-speed switches increase demand for high-layer high-speed PCB.
- 8. Connectors / SerDes / High-speed Interconnect：高拥挤，High-speed signal-chain complexity benefits connectors, SerDes, cables and interconnect solutions.
- 9. Critical Power: UPS / Switchgear / Power Distribution：高拥挤，High-power racks drive UPS, switchgear, power distribution and critical power systems.
- 10. Liquid Cooling / Thermal Management：高拥挤，Higher rack power density drives liquid cooling and thermal management adoption.

**Warming：可继续验证，重点找未拥挤标的**
- 12. Natural Gas Midstream / Pipeline / Compression：中高拥挤，If data centers shift to onsite gas generation, pipeline laterals, compression, storage and firm gas contracts may benefit.
- 14. Copper / Cables / Electrification Metals：中高拥挤，AI data centers, grid expansion and electrification drive copper, cable and electrical materials demand.
- 16. HTCC / Ceramic Packaging Materials：拥挤度尚可，High-reliability ceramic packaging can map to optical communication and CPO upstream materials, but customer proof is needed.
- 19. AI Applications / Agents / ROI：中高拥挤，After hardware crowding, market focus may shift toward application revenue, agent workflows and AI ROI validation.

**Early Signals：不是交易结论，只是人工深挖线索**
- 17. Water / Zero-water Cooling / Treatment：拥挤度尚可，Data center water use, local opposition and regulation can become site-selection constraints, but pure-play mappings are weak.

## 结论

理想目标：逻辑强度高、映射强度高、拥挤度不是高拥挤。若没有完美交集，就按“可研究 / 只能拥挤复核 / 先排除”分层。

**可优先研究：**
- **16. HTCC / Ceramic Packaging Materials**：B层，拥挤度=拥挤度尚可。High-reliability ceramic packaging can map to optical communication and CPO upstream materials, but customer proof is needed.

**只能拥挤复核，不当 early theme：**

- 2. HBM
- 3. DRAM / NAND / Enterprise SSD

- 5. Optical Modules 800G/1.6T
- 6. CPO / Silicon Photonics / OCS
- 7. High-speed PCB
